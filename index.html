Some common questions
which are often asked:

What is our product about?

Our product involves measuring the moisture content in soil using various sensors and techniques.

What are the benefits of our technology?

It optimises irrigation, ensures proper plant growth, and prevents water wastage.

How does our product work?

It typically uses sensors embedded in the soil to measure moisture levels, which are then transmitted to a central system for analysis.

What types of sensors we use in our technology?

Our sensors include capacitance, resistive, and TDR (Time Domain Reflectometry) sensors.

How frequently should soil moisture be monitored?

Regular monitoring, ideally daily or weekly, is recommended.

What is the ideal soil moisture level for plant growth?

Soil moisture level should be kept within a range that promotes healthy root growth and nutrient uptake.
